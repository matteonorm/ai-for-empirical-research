Getting productive with Claude Code means choosing the right subscription tier, setting up a decent terminal environment, and understanding how the context window shapes every interaction. This video walks through installation (npm or standalone), explains auto-compaction and why sessions degrade after too many turns, and compares Claude Code's terminal workflow with Cowork's cloud alternative. The practical tips: be specific, iterate in small steps, correct early when Claude drifts, and always verify research code yourself.
